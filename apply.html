What is |Ns|?

|Ns| stands for Night-Shift gamers. Our well-trained players started to join in 2013. |Ns| has gathered a community of over 40+ people from around the world, although currently, 95% of them are inactive.

If you wish to apply to be part of |Ns|, you're more than welcome to fill out the form on the first page.

By applying to join our team, you will be selected as an admin by default on all of our running servers.

Note: The term "inactive" refers to members who are currently not actively participating in our gaming activities.
